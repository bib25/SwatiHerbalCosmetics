“Health is wealth.”

The phrase “Health is wealth” means that good health is one of the most valuable assets a person can have — even more valuable than money or material possessions. Without good health, it becomes difficult to enjoy life or achieve personal and professional goals. Being healthy enables us to work, play, and live fully, whereas poor health can limit our opportunities and happiness. In essence, this saying reminds us to prioritize taking care of our bodies and minds because no amount of money can replace the benefits of being healthy. Investing time and effort in maintaining physical fitness, eating nutritious foods, managing stress, and getting enough rest is like building true wealth that lasts a lifetime.
